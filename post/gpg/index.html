gpg

2015-02-07 1 min read

文件夹安全权限

chmod 700 .gnupg
chmod -R 600 .gnupg/*

导出公钥

gpg --armor --output public-key.txt --export ACE423E012A77A53BB3531D3B1803BFEC6E78AE5

导出私钥

gpg --armor --output secret-key.txt --export-secret-keys ACE423E012A77A53BB3531D3B1803BFEC6E78AE5

导入密钥

gpg --import ./public-key.txt
gpg --allow-secret-key-import --import ./secret-key.txt
